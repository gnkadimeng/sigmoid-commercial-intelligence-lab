    Why the proposal is Hypothesis v0.1

    The source proposal is a persuasive argument made before the evidence is in. Treating it as truth would bake in untested assumptions.

    So it enters the lab as Hypothesis v0.1: a set of falsifiable claims, each registered and scheduled for validation. Proposal-sourced claims carry low quality and high bias — they can raise a hypothesis, never validate one. See exactly which claims rest on the proposal under Evidence › Hypothesis v0.1.

    What this means in practice. Every score, ranking, and figure on this site is computed from synthetic sample data and carries confidence grade C or D. Nothing is presented as a validated market fact. See the live picture on Current Status.
