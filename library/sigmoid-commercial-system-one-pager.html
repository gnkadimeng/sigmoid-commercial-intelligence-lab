The Sigmoid Commercial System — At a Glance

How the SCS governs our work

One page. Keep it open. The full document is The Sigmoid Commercial System; this is the summary the team works from.

The core idea

We exist to help organisations make better commercial decisions through evidence. Revenue, retention, and growth are all outcomes of commercial decisions.

A business is not a set of departments — it is a living commercial system.

The five commercial questions

Everything we build, say, or measure serves one of these: Find · Win · Keep · Grow customers — and Decide better.

Commercial Health — what we measure

The leading indicator financial statements can’t give you. Seven observable, improvable dimensions:

Lead Quality · Sales Effectiveness · Relationship Quality · Customer Retention · Customer Growth · Revenue Predictability · Decision Quality.

The Commercial Learning Loop — how we improve it

Evidence → Insight → Decision → Action → Outcome → Learning → (better) Evidence

Information tells us what happened; evidence explains why. Businesses that learn faster improve faster. Everything we build exists to make this loop turn faster and more honestly.

Commercial Maturity — where a business is

1 Reactive → 2 Organised → 3 Measured → 4 Evidence-Driven → 5 Commercial Intelligence. It’s about how decisions are made, not company size or software spend.

The ten principles

  1. Businesses are commercial systems. 2. Every outcome is the result of previous decisions.
  2. Better evidence → better decisions. 4. Better decisions → healthier businesses.
  3. Customer relationships are measurable. 6. Follow-up is a competitive advantage.
  4. AI should strengthen human judgement. 8. Revenue is earned through consistent execution.
  5. Learning is a commercial capability. 10. Evidence over opinion.

How everything inherits from the SCS

We do… …relationship to the system
Sigmoid CRM implements the system
Commercial Health / the Index measures the system
Commercial Intelligence Lab evaluates decisions within the system
Content teaches the system
Consulting improves the system
AI augments the system
Research validates and evolves the system

The one test — for anything we build, write, or ship

Does this help someone make a better commercial decision? If yes, it belongs at Sigmoid. If no, it doesn’t.
