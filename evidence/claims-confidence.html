## Claim by claim

For each claim: its declared grade, what real evidence currently exists (from discovery interviews),
and what is still needed to reach grades B and A.

```{python}
#| label: per-claim
#| echo: false
blocks = []
for _, c in claims.iterrows():
    counts = claim_evidence_counts(c["claim_id"])
    if counts["total"] == 0:
        real = '<span class="text-muted-sig">none yet — hypothesis</span>'
    else:
        real = (f'{counts["supports"]} support · {counts["refutes"]} refute · '
                f'{counts["mixed"]} mixed · {counts["neutral"]} neutral')
    blocks.append(
        '<div class="framework-card">'
        '<div class="fc-head">'
        f'<p class="fc-title">{_esc(c["claim_id"])}</p>'
        f'{_grade_chip(c["current_confidence_grade"])}'
        f'<span class="chip chip-neutral no-dot">{_esc(c["decision_id"])} · {_esc(c["claim_type"])}</span>'
        f'<p class="fc-q">{_esc(c["claim_text"])}</p>'
        '</div>'
        '<div class="fc-body">'
        f'<div class="fc-field"><div class="fc-label">Real evidence today</div><div class="fc-val">{real}</div></div>'
        f'<div class="fc-field"><div class="fc-label">Needed for grade B</div><div class="fc-val">{_esc(c["evidence_needed_for_B"])}</div></div>'
        f'<div class="fc-field"><div class="fc-label">Needed for grade A</div><div class="fc-val">{_esc(c["evidence_needed_for_A"])}</div></div>'
        '</div>'
        '</div>'
    )
HTML('<div class="framework-grid">' + "".join(blocks) + "</div>")
```
