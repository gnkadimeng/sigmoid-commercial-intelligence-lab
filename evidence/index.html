The evidence operating layer connects fieldwork to decisions. The pipeline is deliberately simple:

::: {.lifecycle}
<li><span class="stage">Discovery interview</span></li>
<li><span class="arrow">→</span><span class="stage">Structured evidence</span></li>
<li><span class="arrow">→</span><span class="stage">Supports / refutes a claim</span></li>
<li><span class="arrow">→</span><span class="stage">Claim supports a decision</span></li>
<li><span class="arrow">→</span><span class="stage is-loop">Decision status moves</span></li>
:::

A customer interview becomes structured evidence; that evidence supports or refutes a **claim**; that
claim supports a **decision**; and the decision shows whether we are still at hypothesis stage or
moving toward a recommendation.

#| label: layer-metrics

import sys, pathlib
_root = pathlib.Path.cwd()
while _root != _root.parent and not (_root / "src").is_dir():

    + metric("Real evidence records", len(evidence), "mapped to claims")
    + "</div>"
)

## The templates

Collecting real evidence uses two operator templates in `templates/`:

- **Discovery interview** — run and log an interview → one row in `data/raw/discovery_interviews.csv`.
- **Evidence capture** — turn that interview into evidence records → rows in
  `data/processed/discovery_evidence.csv`, each linked to a claim and a decision.
